Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) ratings are collected automatically when a ticket is resolved. Customers receive a prompt asking them to rate their support experience from 1 to 5 stars.

How CSAT works
  • An agent resolves a support ticket.
  • The customer receives a rating email.
  • They click a star (1–5) to submit feedback.
  • Ratings appear here in real time.
